Our Mission

Our mission is to empower you with the tools and guidance to design your own home storage, so your space becomes a personalized corner market, filled with the things you use and love.

During the Great Recession beginning in 2008, we gained a deeper understanding of the value of home storage—discovering firsthand what works well and what doesn’t. Since then, we’ve dedicated ourselves to developing a storage system for food and essentials tailored to our unique preferences, lifestyle, and family needs that is constantly rotating and where nothing goes to waste. Through this website and our companion app, we are excited to share the experiences and practical solutions we have learned, hoping they will help you create a home storage system that supports your daily life and is there for you in times of need.

The Home Corner Market App

Just enter your favorite recipes, including the ingredient amounts and how often you prepare each dish over a three-month period. The app will then calculate your usage based on these frequencies plus any additional three-month consumption of individual items, generating a comprehensive list of everything you need.
